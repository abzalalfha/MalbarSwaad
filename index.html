Our Roots

A Recipe Passed Down Through Generations

"Every chip carries the memory of my grandmother's hands, the fragrance of her kitchen, and the love she stirred into every batch."

MalbarSwaad was born in a small kitchen in Kozhikode, Kerala — where recipes were never written but whispered from mother to daughter. Our founder, inspired by the rich snack traditions of Malabar, set out to revive these treasures for modern India.

From the golden banana chips fried in pure coconut oil, to the fiery crunch of Payyoli Mixture — every product we make is a tribute to authentic craftsmanship. No shortcuts. No artificial flavours. Just pure, honest tradition.

"Every tradition worth keeping deserves to be tasted by the next generation."

Our Beginning

Rooted in Malabar's Rich Tradition

MalbarSwaad was born in 2012 in the fragrant lanes of Kozhikode — a city whose name itself is synonymous with spice trade and culinary richness. Our founder, Abdulla Rahman, watched his mother fry banana chips every festival season and knew the world deserved to taste that same love.

What began as weekend batches gifted to neighbours slowly grew into something extraordinary. By 2015, the first packets of MalbarSwaad were being sold at local markets. By 2020, we had orders from Mumbai. Today, we serve families across India — but every chip still carries the spirit of that first Kozhikode kitchen.

"We don't make snacks. We make memories in a bag."

— Abdulla Rahman, Founder

2012
The First Batch

Abdulla's mother's banana chip recipe, first made for a Eid gathering in Kozhikode. 50 packets, sold out in an hour.

2015
MalbarSwaad is Born

Brand officially registered. First commercial kitchen set up in Kozhikode with 3 employees. Payyoli Mixture added to the range.

2018
Certified Premium

FSSAI certification obtained. ISO-standard production facility commissioned. 10,000+ customers milestone reached.

2020
Mumbai Expansion

First cross-state delivery. Retail tie-up with 5 premium Mumbai gourmet stores. Online store launched.

2025
Pan-India Vision

50,000+ customers. Bangalore added. Plans for Delhi, Chennai and franchise model underway. The journey continues.
